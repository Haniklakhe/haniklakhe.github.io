Focus Areas

What I Study & Explore

Hydrological Modelling
Flood risk, water security frameworks and watershed management using SWAT, HEC-HMS, and MIKE+.
Remote Sensing & GIS
Satellite-based climate risk and CWIS sanitation mapping using ArcGIS, QGIS, and Google Earth Engine.
AI/ML in Hydrology
Machine learning for extreme event analysis, CMIP6 temperature forecasting, and landslide susceptibility mapping.
Sediment Dynamics
Suspended sediment flux in Himalayan rivers using citizen science, OBS sensors, and real-time turbidity monitoring.
Nature-Based Solutions
Urban water management NBS frameworks and best practices for sustainable Asian city infrastructure.
Citizen Science
Community-based water monitoring networks; evaluating citizen scientist reliability vs standard datasets.
Climate Change Adaptation
CMIP6 extreme projections, disaster resilience, and post-disaster management for informal settlements.
Water & Sanitation (WASH)
CWIS city assessments, geospatial sanitation planning, and equitable water infrastructure in Asia.
